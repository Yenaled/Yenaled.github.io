Welcome!

This website is rich in information pertaining to DNA sequencing. It focuses specifically on the Sanger Method or the Dideoxy Chain Termination Method, which was the primary method of DNA sequencing for over 25 years. This website includes a brief history of this sequencing method, a concise but easy-to-follow explanation of how this method works, and finally, interactive activities to solidify one's conceptual understanding of this method. This website strives to explain DNA sequencing in a unique and interactive fashion, and I hope that users will leave the site with a greater appreciation and deeper understanding of DNA sequencing.
